About

Mashówski is a Setúbal–based business working at the intersection of analog perceptual design, color–driven neuro-sensory interfaces, geometrical string art and spatial installations.

Every piece is built by hand and designed specifically for the space, the light and the emotional tone of the environment. The studio approach combines aesthetics, neuroscience and spatial logic: form, rhythm, color, perception.

Practive Areas

Perceptual Recalibration Interfaces (PRIs)

Neuro-sensory analog tools for regulating focus, emotional tone and spatial presence.
Available in custom sizes (40×40 cm to 150×150 cm) and tailored color palettes.

Check PRI manual for private spaces, studios, hospitality venues, wellness centers, therapeutic and autism–friendly environments here: EN or PT

String Art Objects & Wall Sculptures

Geometric, asymmetric or spiral-based string structures designed for private homes, studios, hotels and public interiors. Each object functions as both visual anchor and perceptual instrument.
Available in custom sizes and tailored color palettes.

Site Visits & Spatial Assessment

Available in Lisbon and Setúbal area. Other areas on demand. On-site sessions include:

  • assessment of light and perceptual flows,
  • format and scale recommendations,
  • palette definition for the emotional tone of the space,
  • clarifying the function of each object in daily use.
Commissions & Collaborative Projects

The studio accepts bespoke requests for:

  • large-scale installations,
  • color architecture experiments,
  • perceptual environments,
  • therapy-oriented sensory objects,
  • collaborations with designers, architects and curators.

How Commissions Work

  1. Initial contact. You briefly describe your space, goals and desired size range.
  2. On-site assessment (optional). A visit to understand light, movement and emotional context.
  3. Concept and palette. You receive one to three concepts with formats, colors and perceptual function.
  4. Production. Each piece is built entirely by hand. Typical production time is one to three weeks, depending on scale and complexity.
  5. Delivery and installation. Safe mounting solutions are included or discussed individually.
Every project is made to order. No two pieces are identical.
